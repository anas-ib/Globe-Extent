# Contact & Lead Generation

The website encourages visitors to contact Globe Extent LLP rather than purchase services online.

Every service should provide a clear call-to-action directing users to the shared Contact page.

Recommended CTA labels:

- Talk to an Expert
- Book a Meeting
- Request Consultation
- Contact Our Team
- Explore Service

The Contact page dynamically updates its heading based on the selected service while maintaining the same layout.

---

# Content Style

Keep content concise, professional and easy to understand.

Focus on business value, trust and credibility.

Avoid unnecessary technical language and long paragraphs.

The website should position Globe Extent LLP as a reliable long-term business partner with expertise across consulting, technology, products and event management.

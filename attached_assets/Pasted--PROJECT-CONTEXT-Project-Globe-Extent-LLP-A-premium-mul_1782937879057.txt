# PROJECT CONTEXT

## Project

Globe Extent LLP

A premium multi-page corporate website representing Globe Extent LLP and its business divisions.

The website is designed for lead generation, brand positioning, and client acquisition.

Visitors should explore services and contact Globe Extent LLP for consultation through WhatsApp, phone, email, or a contact form.

This is not an e-commerce platform or SaaS application.

---

# Website Structure

The website follows a multi-page architecture.

Pages:

- Home
- Business Solutions
- Software Services
- Products
- Event Management
- About Us
- Contact Us

Each business division has its own dedicated page.

The Home page introduces the company and provides a preview of the four business divisions.

It should never contain every service in detail.

Every business page has its own hero section, content structure, imagery, and call-to-action.

---

# Development Workflow

Build the website incrementally.

Development order:

1. Foundation
2. Home
3. Business Solutions
4. Software Services
5. Products
6. Event Management
7. About Us
8. Contact Us
9. Final Polish

Work only on the requested page.

Previously completed pages should remain visually consistent while allowing improvements that enhance the overall experience.

---

# Contact Flow

Every service across the website leads to the same Contact page.

The Contact page should dynamically update its Hero title, subtitle and selected service reference depending on the page from which the visitor arrives.

The layout remains identical.

---

# Technology

Choose the best technology stack to achieve the highest quality result.

The stack may be upgraded whenever it improves the user experience, animations, maintainability or performance.

Preferred stack:

- React
- Vite
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- React Router
- Framer Motion
- Lenis Smooth Scroll
- Lucide React

Additional libraries may be introduced if they significantly improve the experience.

---

# Overall Goal

The website should feel like it was designed and developed by a premium digital agency.

The experience should be modern, interactive, elegant and memorable while maintaining a professional corporate identity.

Animations, transitions and micro-interactions are an essential part of the project and should never be simplified or removed merely to reduce implementation complexity.

The final website should prioritize:

- Premium design
- Excellent responsiveness
- Smooth interactions
- Performance
- Accessibility
- Modern frontend practices
- Reusable components
- Clean architecture
